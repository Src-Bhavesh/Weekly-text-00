Who We Are

Luxor Emirates is a premier real estate brokerage headquartered in Dubai, specializing in the buying, selling, and leasing of luxury residential and commercial properties. Since our inception in 2008, we have been committed to delivering exceptional service and unmatched expertise in the UAE property market.


Our portfolio includes some of the most sought-after addresses in the city, from private islands to penthouses with skyline views. We pride ourselves on our integrity, transparency, and dedication to our clients' needs.


Whether you are looking to invest in off-plan projects or acquire a ready villa, our team of experts is here to guide you through every step of the process.
